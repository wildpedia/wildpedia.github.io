About AnimalKingdom

AnimalKingdom is a free, open-source platform helping people discover and appreciate the animal kingdom.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding animals — their strengths, senses, and roles in nature — is the first step toward protecting them. AnimalKingdom makes animal knowledge accessible, visual, and engaging for everyone.

Our Data

All data comes from reputable scientific sources including IUCN Red List, Animal Diversity Web, National Geographic, and WWF. We strive for accuracy but recommend consulting primary sources for critical research.

Open Source

AnimalKingdom is open source and free to use. Built with vanilla JavaScript, static JSON data, and Chart.js. No frameworks, no tracking cookies, no ads.
